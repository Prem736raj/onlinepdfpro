    Frequently Asked Questions

    Q. Do I need to remove data URI prefix manually?

    No. The converter automatically strips prefixes like data:application/pdf;base64,.

    Q. Why does conversion fail for some strings?

    Invalid Base64 or non-PDF/non-image binary payload will trigger validation errors.

    Q. Can I output MIME chunked Base64?

    Yes. Enable the 76-character chunk option in the PDF-to-Base64 panel.

    Q. Are JPG and PNG Base64 values supported?

    Yes. Auto mode detects and previews both PNG and JPEG image payloads.

    Q. Is this converter private?

    Yes. All decoding and encoding happens locally in your browser only.
