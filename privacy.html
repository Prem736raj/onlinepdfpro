    Last updated: February 2026

    Your files never leave your device. We don't collect, store, or transmit any of your data.

    1. Introduction

    OnlinePDFPro ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our website at onlinepdfpro.com.

    2. File Processing

    All file processing occurs entirely in your web browser. When you use any of our tools:

    • Your files are never uploaded to our servers or any third-party servers
    • Files are processed using JavaScript that runs locally on your device
    • We have no access to your files or their contents
    • Processed files exist only in your browser's memory until you download them or close the tab

    3. Local Storage

    We use your browser's local storage to save:

    • Theme preference: Whether you prefer light or dark mode
    • Recently used tools: A list of tools you've recently accessed for convenience

    This data is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted to us.

    4. Cookies

    OnlinePDFPro does not use cookies for tracking or analytics. The only data stored in your browser is as described in the Local Storage section above.

    5. Analytics

    We do not use any analytics services, tracking pixels, or similar technologies. We do not collect information about your browsing behavior, device, or location.

    6. Third-Party Libraries

    Our tools use open-source JavaScript libraries loaded from CDNs (Content Delivery Networks). These libraries may log access requests as part of their normal operation. We recommend reviewing the privacy policies of:

    • unpkg.com (CDN for npm packages)
    • Google Fonts (font delivery)

    7. Hosting

    This website is hosted on GitHub Pages. GitHub may collect basic server logs as part of their service. Please refer to GitHub's Privacy Statement for more information.

    8. Children's Privacy

    Our service is available to users of all ages. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from anyone.

    9. Changes to This Policy

    We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.

    10. Contact

    If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through our GitHub repository.
