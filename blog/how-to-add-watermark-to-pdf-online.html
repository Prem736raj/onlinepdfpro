Why Use PDF Watermarks?

  • Document status: Mark documents as DRAFT, SAMPLE, APPROVED, or FINAL to prevent confusion
  • Confidentiality: Stamp CONFIDENTIAL or RESTRICTED to remind handlers of sensitivity
  • Copyright protection: Add your name or company logo to prevent unauthorized redistribution
  • Version control: Date-stamp versions to track document iterations
  • Legal compliance: Some industries require documents to be watermarked before external distribution

Types of Watermarks

Text Watermarks

Common options: DRAFT, CONFIDENTIAL, SAMPLE, DO NOT COPY, APPROVED, VOID. Customize opacity (usually 15-30% for readability), color (gray for subtle, red for urgent), and rotation (45° diagonal is standard).

Image Watermarks

Upload your company logo as a PNG (with transparency) for brand protection. Logo watermarks work well at low opacity across the center of each page.

Best Practices

Keep Opacity Low

Watermarks should be visible but not obstruct the document's readability. 15-25% opacity works well for most documents.

Use Diagonal Placement

A 45° rotated watermark across the center of the page is the industry standard. It's visible on every page without interfering with content flow.

Flatten After Watermarking

For security-critical watermarks, flatten the PDF after watermarking to prevent removal.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Can the watermark be removed by recipients?▼

Standard watermarks can be removed with PDF editing tools. For tamper-resistant watermarks, flatten the PDF after adding the watermark.

Will the watermark appear when printing?▼

Yes. Watermarks are embedded in the PDF and will appear in printed copies.

Can I watermark only specific pages?▼

The current tool applies watermarks to all pages. For selective watermarking, split the PDF, watermark the needed pages, then merge.

Is my document uploaded to a server?▼

No. Watermarking happens entirely in your browser. Your document stays private.

Can I use my company logo as a watermark?▼

Yes. Upload any PNG or JPG image as a watermark. PNG with transparent background works best.
