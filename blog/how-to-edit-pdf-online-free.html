What You Can Edit

  • Add text: Insert new text anywhere on the page — fill in forms, add missing info, or annotate
  • Highlight text: Mark important passages in yellow, green, or custom colors
  • Draw freehand: Circle areas, draw arrows, or mark up diagrams
  • Add images: Insert logos, stamps, or visual annotations
  • Sign documents: Draw or type your signature directly on the PDF
  • Whiteout content: Cover sensitive information with a white rectangle

Editing Tips

Match Existing Fonts

When adding text to fill in a form, try to match the font size and style of the surrounding text for a professional look.

Use Whiteout for Corrections

To "erase" existing content, add a white rectangle over the area, then add your corrected text on top. This technique is how most free PDF editors handle content replacement.

Flatten Before Sharing

After editing, use our Flatten PDF tool to permanently merge your edits into the document. This prevents recipients from moving or removing your annotations.

Choosing the Right Editing Approach

  • Minor additions (text, signatures): Use the PDF Editor directly
  • Major text changes: Convert to Word, edit in Word, convert back to PDF
  • Form filling: Use the PDF Editor's text tool to type directly in form fields
  • Replacing images: Use the PDF Editor to add new images over existing ones

🔒 Privacy

Your PDF is processed entirely in your browser. No files are uploaded to any server. This makes it safe to edit contracts, financial documents, and personal files.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Can I edit existing text in the PDF?▼

You can add new text, but modifying existing text (reflowing paragraphs) requires professional tools like Adobe Acrobat. For significant text changes, convert to Word first, edit, then convert back.

Will a watermark be added?▼

No. OnlinePDFPro never adds watermarks to your documents.

Does it work on mobile?▼

Yes. The editor works on mobile browsers. Use your finger to draw, sign, or place text.

Is there a file size limit?▼

No server-side limit. Very large files (100MB+) may load slowly on older devices since processing is local.

Can I undo changes?▼

Yes. The editor supports undo/redo during your editing session.
