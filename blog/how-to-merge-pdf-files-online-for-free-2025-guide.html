❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Is there a limit to how many PDFs I can merge?▼

No hard limit. You can merge as many files as your device can handle. We recommend batches of 20 or fewer for optimal performance.

Will the merged PDF preserve bookmarks?▼

The current tool focuses on page content. Bookmarks from individual files may not be preserved. Use our PDF Bookmarks tool to add navigation after merging.

Can I merge PDFs on my phone?▼

Yes. The merge tool works on all mobile browsers. Upload files and drag to reorder using touch gestures.

Are my files uploaded to a server?▼

No. Merging happens entirely in your browser using the pdf-lib JavaScript library. Your documents never leave your device.

Can I merge PDFs with different page sizes?▼

Yes. Files with different page sizes (A4, Letter, custom) can be merged. Each page retains its original dimensions in the combined PDF.
