Why You Don't Need Adobe Acrobat

Adobe Acrobat became the standard PDF editor because it was the first — Adobe invented the PDF format in 1993. But three decades later, the landscape has changed dramatically. Modern web technologies like JavaScript, Canvas API, and WebAssembly can handle sophisticated PDF manipulation directly in your browser. Here's what you can do without Adobe:

  • Add, edit, and delete text
  • Highlight, underline, and strikethrough text
  • Draw freehand annotations
  • Insert shapes (rectangles, circles, arrows)
  • Add electronic signatures
  • Insert images and stamps
  • Whiteout/redact sensitive information

What Can You Edit in a PDF?

Text Annotations

Add new text anywhere on the page. This is ideal for filling in forms, adding notes, or inserting missing information. While you can't directly modify existing text in most free editors (this requires advanced text reflow capabilities), you can add text boxes that overlay or replace content.

Visual Markup

Highlight important passages in yellow, red, green, or custom colors. Add underlines for emphasis or strikethrough for deletions. These tools are essential for document review and feedback.

Signatures

Draw your signature with a mouse or touchscreen, type it, or upload a signature image. Our Sign PDF tool makes this simple.

Form Filling

Fill out PDF forms by clicking on form fields and typing. For non-interactive forms (where there are no fillable fields), use the text tool to type directly on the form.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Can I edit existing text in a PDF?▼

You can add new text and overlay content. Directly modifying existing text (reflow editing) requires advanced software like Adobe Acrobat Pro.

Will the edited PDF have a watermark?▼

No. OnlinePDFPro never adds watermarks to your documents. The output is clean and professional.

Can I edit PDFs on my phone?▼

Yes. Our PDF editor works on mobile browsers. Tap to add text, use your finger to draw or sign.

Is there a file size limit?▼

No server-side limit since everything processes locally. Very large PDFs (100MB+) may be slow on older devices.

Can I undo changes?▼

Yes. The editor supports undo/redo. You can also download at any point to save a checkpoint.
