Why Save WhatsApp Chats as PDF?

  • Legal evidence: Courts in many countries accept WhatsApp chat exports as evidence. A PDF provides a tamper-resistant, dated record
  • Business records: Preserve client conversations, project discussions, and agreements for future reference
  • Personal backup: Keep memorable conversations safe even if you change phones or delete the app
  • Sharing with non-WhatsApp users: Share conversation content with people who don't use WhatsApp
  • Academic documentation: Save research discussions, interview transcripts, or group project conversations

How to Export WhatsApp Chat on Android

1

Open the Chat

Open the specific WhatsApp conversation you want to export.

2

Access Chat Settings

Tap the three-dot menu (⋮) in the top-right corner → select "More" → tap "Export chat".

3

Choose With or Without Media

Select "Without media" for a text-only export (smaller file) or "Include media" to include shared photos and videos.

4

Save the Export

Choose where to save the .txt file — Google Drive, email, or your device storage. This .txt file is what you'll convert to PDF.

How to Export WhatsApp Chat on iPhone

1

Open the Chat

Open the conversation in WhatsApp.

2

Tap the Contact Name

Tap the contact or group name at the top to open chat info.

3

Export Chat

Scroll down and tap "Export Chat". Choose "Without Media" or "Attach Media".

4

Save or Share

Use the iOS share sheet to save the .txt file to Files, email it to yourself, or AirDrop it to your computer.

Tips for Better Documentation

Export Individual Chats Separately

Export each important conversation as its own PDF rather than trying to combine everything. This makes finding specific conversations easier.

Add Date References

WhatsApp exports include timestamps. When converting to PDF, keep these timestamps visible as they serve as important chronological markers.

For Legal Use

If the chat export is for legal purposes, export without editing the text. Include the full conversation with timestamps. Some jurisdictions require screenshots as additional verification alongside text exports.

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Can I export group chat conversations?▼

Yes. The export process is the same for individual and group chats. Group exports include the sender name for each message.

Will images be included in the PDF?▼

If you export "With Media," images are saved as separate files alongside the .txt file. You can add them to the PDF using our Images to PDF tool.

Can I export WhatsApp Business chats?▼

Yes. WhatsApp Business has the same export functionality as regular WhatsApp.

Is there a message limit for exports?▼

WhatsApp exports up to 40,000 messages without media, or 10,000 messages with media. Older messages beyond this limit are not included.

Can I convert the chat directly on my phone?▼

Yes. Open OnlinePDFPro in your mobile browser. All our tools work on mobile devices without needing an app.
