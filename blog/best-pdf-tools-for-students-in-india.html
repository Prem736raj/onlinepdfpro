PDF Tasks Every Student Needs

  • Compress PDFs: Exam portals (UPSC, GATE, NEET, JEE) have strict 50KB-200KB limits for document uploads
  • Merge PDFs: Combine multiple assignment pages or project documents into a single submission file
  • Convert images to PDF: Turn handwritten notes, photos of whiteboard, or phone scans into neat PDF documents
  • Split PDFs: Extract specific pages from textbooks or reference materials
  • OCR (Text extraction): Convert scanned textbook pages into selectable, searchable text for notes
  • PDF to Word: Edit content from existing PDFs for papers and reports

Indian Exam Portal Size Requirements

  • GATE: Photo (under 480×640, max 200KB), Signature (max 80KB)
  • UPSC: Photo JPG (20-300KB), Signature JPG (10-40KB), Documents PDF (max 300KB each)
  • NEET: Photo (10-200KB), Signature (4-30KB)
  • JEE Main: Photo JPG (10-200KB), Signature (4-30KB)
  • CAT: Photo (max 5MB), Signature (max 5MB)
  • IBPS: Photo (20-50KB), Signature (10-20KB)

The Student Assignment Workflow

Step 1: Write and Scan

Write your assignment, then scan each page using your phone camera or a scanner app.

Step 2: Convert to PDF

Use Images to PDF to compile all scanned pages into a single PDF. Arrange pages in correct order.

Step 3: Add Cover Page

Create a cover page (can be a simple Word document converted to PDF). Then merge the cover page with your assignment.

Step 4: Compress if Needed

If the submission portal has a size limit, compress the final document.
