Frequently Asked Questions

Q. Can I unlock any PDF?

You need to know the current password. This tool removes protection from PDFs you have authorized access to — it is not a password cracker.

Q. Is my password safe?

Yes! Your password is never sent to any server. The decryption happens entirely in your browser using pdf-lib.

Q. Does it work on mobile?

Yes! Works perfectly on all devices — Android, iPhone, tablets, and desktops. No app needed.
